Isaac Lewin is a Founding Member at ShopGuide, where he leads agentic web development for both the company and the merchants we support. His background combines direct sales, marketing agency experience, and hands-on AI implementation for Shopify brands.

**Specialty** – Isaac blends strategic thinking with practical execution, helping merchants turn agentic commerce into measurable business outcomes. He provides actionable insights that drive growth for both merchant partners and ShopGuide.

**Vision** – After installing ShopGuide on the <a href="https://countrylifefoods.com" className="text-primary-500 hover:text-primary-600 dark:text-primary-500 dark:hover:text-primary-400">Country Life Natural Foods</a> website, Isaac immediately saw how powerful agentic systems can be for high-catalog stores. He now focuses on scaling that impact: supporting merchant success, strengthening ShopGuide's strategic direction, and leading partnerships with leading Shopify agencies and app partners.

Isaac writes about agentic commerce, AI-driven customer experiences, and the future of e-commerce on the ShopGuide blog.
