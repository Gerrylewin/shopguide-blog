**Specialty** – Isaac specializes in the **Shopify Catalog API** and **Liquid optimization**, ensuring that agentic commerce systems run at peak performance even for stores with 10,000+ SKUs.

**Vision** – Since implementing the first autonomous agent on <a href="https://countrylifefoods.com" className="text-primary-500 hover:text-primary-600 dark:text-primary-500 dark:hover:text-primary-400">Country Life Natural Foods</a>, Isaac has been on a mission to kill the traditional search bar. He works closely with Shopify Plus agencies to deploy agents that don't just answer questions, but drive revenue.

Isaac is a recognized voice in the Shopify ecosystem, writing frequently about **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** and the future of **Autonomous Retail**.
