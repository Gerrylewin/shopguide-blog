## The Filter Wall is Real

In a specialty food store with 10,000+ SKUs, the "Search and Filter" experience is often where revenue goes to die. We call this the **Dietary Discovery Gap**.

Traditional [Shopify](https://shopify.pxf.io/eK6XxX) storefronts rely on faceted search—those long lists of checkboxes on the left side of the screen. While they work for simple queries like "Gluten-Free," they crumble under the weight of real-world human intent. A customer doesn't just want "Vegan"; they want "a low-acid coffee substitute that is organic and doesn't contain chicory."

On a legacy site, that customer has to click five filters, read three product descriptions, and pray they didn't miss a hidden ingredient. Most of the time, they just leave.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Why do standard Shopify filters fail for high-SKU food brands?**
Standard filters are rigid. They require a merchant to manually tag every product with every possible dietary attribute. In a 10,000+ SKU catalog, tags are often inconsistent or missing ("Dirty Data"). Furthermore, filters can't handle complex, multi-layered queries like "organic snacks that are kid-friendly but don't contain sunflower oil." Agentic reasoning bypasses these limits by reading the full product description and metafields in real-time.

**How does ShopGuide handle 'Dirty Data' in large catalogs?**
Unlike legacy search which needs perfect tagging, ShopGuide uses semantic embeddings. This means the AI understands the _meaning_ of your product descriptions. If a description mentions "stone-ground heritage wheat," the agent knows it belongs in a search for "ancient grains" even if the merchant forgot to add that specific tag.

**What is the difference between a chatbot and an agentic shopping assistant?**
A chatbot is a retrieval tool; you ask a question, it gives an answer from a script. An agentic assistant like ShopGuide _reasons_. It can look at a customer's cart, check inventory levels via the [Shopify](https://shopify.pxf.io/eK6XxX) Catalog API, and suggest a bulk-sized alternative to save the customer money—all while maintaining a natural conversation.

**Can ShopGuide help reduce 'Search Abandonment' in food stores?**
Yes. High-SKU stores often suffer from "Zero Results" pages when a user types a query slightly differently than the product title. Because ShopGuide understands intent, it eliminates the dead-end search. If a customer searches for "morning coffee alternative," the agent knows to surface products like [Organic Dandy Blend](https://countrylifefoods.com/collections/coffee-substitutes/products/organic-dandy-blend-instant-beverage) instead of showing an empty page.

**How long does it take to 'train' the agent on a 10,000 SKU catalog?**
With ShopGuide, there is no manual training. By plugging directly into the [Shopify](https://shopify.pxf.io/eK6XxX) Catalog API, the agent "reads" and indexes your entire store in minutes.
