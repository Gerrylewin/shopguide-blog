## The AOV Ceiling in Large Catalogs

For merchants with a large number of SKUs, increasing Average Order Value (AOV) is a unique challenge. You have the inventory to support massive carts, but customers often buy only the single item they came for because they don't know what else you offer.

"Customers also bought" widgets are often too generic to be effective in a specialized catalog. If you're selling hundreds of varieties of organic grains—like the experts at [Country Life Natural Foods](https://countrylifefoods.com)—a generic algorithm simply won't cut it.

## Introducing Agentic Upselling

Agentic commerce moves beyond the "You might also like" algorithm. It introduces **Guided Shopping**.

By integrating with [**Alia**](https://apps.shopify.com/alia?mref=cxnuusor) for personalization and **Judge.me** for social proof, ShopGuide acts as a consultant. This approach has already helped our merchant network generate over **$250,000 in revenue**.

### How Guided Shopping Boosts Cart Size:

1.  **Contextual Bundling:** If a customer is buying bulk oats, ShopGuide doesn't just suggest "more oats." It suggests specific dried fruits and seeds that pair perfectly, explaining _why_ they go together.
2.  **Building Buyer Confidence:** Customers spend more when they are certain they've made the right choice. By answering technical questions, ShopGuide removes the "uncertainty gap."
3.  **Real-Time Cross-Selling:** Because the agent is "catalog-aware," it can spot opportunities that a static widget misses.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does an AI agent increase AOV differently than a recommendation widget?**
A recommendation widget uses historical data to show what _other_ people bought. An AI agent uses real-time conversation to understand what _this_ customer needs right now, providing contextual advice that builds confidence.

**How does "Buyer Confidence" affect my store's revenue?**
One of the biggest reasons for low AOV is "product uncertainty." By providing instant, expert answers, ShopGuide increases confidence, leading to larger bulk orders and more comprehensive carts.

**What kind of AOV increase can merchants expect?**
While results vary, merchants using agentic discovery typically see a 15-25% increase in Average Order Value within the first 60 days.
