Most Shopify merchants with big catalogs know the feeling.

Traffic comes in. People browse. They add a couple things. Then they bounce because finding the exact product they need feels like digging through a warehouse blindfolded.

You’ve got the inventory. The problem is getting customers to it.

That’s where ShopGuide comes in. It’s not a popup chatbot. It’s an on-page AI agent that lives right on your product pages, collections, and everywhere else. It uses Shopify’s native Catalog API to know your entire store—variants, metafields, stock—and guides people to what they actually want.

## What it does (straight from how it works)

ShopGuide embeds directly into your theme. No heavy scripts slowing things down. Once set up:

- It answers questions using your real product data.
- It recommends based on intent, not just "people also bought."
- It handles the full journey—discovery to cart add.
- Merchants see attributed revenue and orders in the dashboard.

From case studies: assisted orders show 19-48% higher AOV. One brand hit +19.6%, another +39%. Not hype numbers—real attributed sales from traffic you already pay for.

Setup is simple. Install from the Shopify App Store, point it at your theme via the editor (drag the block where you want), do a quick training pass on shipping/returns if needed, and it syncs. Premium gets full hand-holding. It matches your brand with custom CSS. No rebuilding your store.

## What merchants actually complain about

Reddit and forums are full of the same gripes: cart abandonment, discovery friction in big catalogs, too many half-baked apps, support overload.

High-SKU stores especially feel it. Filters break. Search misses. Long-tail products sit invisible while bestsellers carry everything.

ShopGuide fixes the core issue: turning passive browsers into guided buyers without adding another layer of complexity. It’s agentic—it acts, reasons over your catalog, and closes gaps that traditional tools don’t touch.

One founder put it well: merchants want tools that handle the messy reality of large inventories without more manual work. This does that.

## Honest value

It won’t 10x your store overnight. But for stores with 1,000+ products struggling with findability, it surfaces hidden inventory, builds confidence, and lifts AOV on the sessions where it engages (around 5% of visitors per their data).

Revenue attribution shows what’s working. You keep control—no black box.

If your catalog feels like it’s working against you instead of for you, this is worth testing. Free to install, quick to see results.

**Will it slow my store?**  
No. Native and lightweight. Merchants report clean performance.

**Best for what kind of stores?**  
High-SKU: industrial parts, hobby/collectibles, food/supplements, apparel with variants, etc. Where discovery is the bottleneck.

**Support tickets?**  
Many see reduction because the agent handles common questions and even post-purchase tweaks in the action window.
