## The Veterinary Catalog Crisis: Too Much of a Good Thing

Pet care is increasingly specialized. We no longer just buy "dog food"; we buy grain-free, hypoallergenic, small-breed puppy formulas with added glucosamine for joint health. When a catalog grows to thousands of SKUs, traditional navigation breaks down.

The "5,000 SKU Filter Wall" is particularly high in the veterinary space. A customer might need to filter by species, age, health condition, ingredient restrictions, and brand. If they miss a single checkbox, the product they need disappears from view.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does an AI agent handle prescription-only pet products?**
The agent uses the Shopify Catalog API to identify products tagged as "prescription" or "veterinary exclusive." It can then guide the customer through the necessary verification steps or explain the requirements for purchase, ensuring compliance while maintaining a smooth shopping flow.

**Can the agent distinguish between different breed-specific needs?**
Yes. By indexing detailed product descriptions and metafields, the agent can recognize that a Great Dane has different nutritional and orthopedic requirements than a French Bulldog. It can then surface breed-specific gear and food without the customer having to navigate multiple sub-categories.

**Does agentic commerce help reduce returns for pet supplies?**
Significantly. Most pet supply returns happen because of incorrect sizing (for harnesses/apparel) or the pet refusing a specific food type. By asking clarifying questions about the pet's size, age, and preferences before the purchase, the agent ensures a higher "first-time right" rate.

**Is it possible to set a time limit for order changes on pet supplies?**
Yes. Merchants can utilize the **Action Window** in the ShopGuide dashboard to allow customers to [edit orders after purchase](/blog/reduce-support-tickets-with-order-edits). This is particularly useful if an owner realizes they ordered the wrong flavor or variant of a health-critical product.

**How does ShopGuide handle out-of-stock items in a large veterinary catalog?**
ShopGuide performs live inventory checks via the Shopify API. If a specific renal diet is out of stock, the agent won't just say "No." It will analyze the nutritional profile and suggest the closest compatible alternative that is currently available, preserving the sale and helping the customer's pet.
