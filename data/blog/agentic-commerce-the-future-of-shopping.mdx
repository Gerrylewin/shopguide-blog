## The Death of the Chatbot

If you're still calling your AI a "chatbot," you've already lost.

In the old days—like, way back in 2024—merchants were obsessed with "conversational commerce." The goal was to have a bubble in the corner of the screen that could answer FAQs and maybe give a discount code. It was passive. It was slow. And honestly? It was annoying.

Welcome to 2026. We've moved past conversation. We’re now in the **Age of Execution**.

## What is Agentic Commerce?

Agentic Commerce isn't just a buzzword; it’s a fundamental shift in how the internet works. Instead of a customer doing all the heavy lifting—searching, filtering, comparing, and eventually clicking "checkout"—an **AI Agent** does the work for them.
