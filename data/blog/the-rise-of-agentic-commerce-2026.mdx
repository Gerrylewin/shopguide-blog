## Beyond the Bubble: The End of "Chat"

For years, e-commerce was obsessed with "conversational commerce." We all remember the 2024 era: a small chat bubble in the corner of every Shopify store that could answer "Where is my order?" or provide a generic discount code. It was a improvement over static FAQs, but it was still inherently passive.

Welcome to **2026**. The conversation is over. The execution has begun.

We have officially entered the era of **Agentic Commerce**—a paradigm shift where AI doesn't just talk to your customers; it works for them.

## The Industry Visionaries Have Spoken

This isn't just another tech trend. The leaders of the commerce world have been signaling this shift for the past year.

**Tobi Lütke**, CEO of **Shopify**, recently [emphasized on X](https://liquidweekly.com/blogs/newsletter/issue-195-agentic-commerce-has-arrived):

> "Agents will become a common way people shop. We're making that as easy as possible for the AI age."

It’s not just about the storefront. It’s about the entire financial infrastructure. **John Collison**, co-founder of **Stripe**, [predicted](https://www.theblock.co/post/391244/stripe-co-founder-predicts-torrent-of-ai-agent-commerce-powered-by-stablecoins) a "torrent of agentic commerce," highlighting how autonomous agents will soon handle the majority of transactional volume.

Perhaps most tellingly, **Harley Finkelstein**, President of **Shopify**, [shared in a recent interview](https://www.retailbrew.com/stories/2026/01/16/shopify-president-agentic-commerce-could-usher-in-merit-based-shopping) that retail is entering its "agentic era," where AI agents will level the playing field for brands by handling complex discovery and checkout processes autonomously.

The difference between a 2024 chatbot and a 2026 AI Agent comes down to one word: **Agency**.

While a chatbot is restricted to a pre-defined decision tree, an AI Agent like **ShopGuide** has the autonomy to use tools, access real-time data, and complete multi-step tasks without human intervention.
