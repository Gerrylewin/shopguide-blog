### The On-Page Difference That Actually Moves the Needle

Traditional search and filter apps are **passive**. They wait for the shopper to type the exact right words or click the right narrow filters. In a large or complex catalog, that breaks down fast. Zero results. Wrong results. Choice overload. Shoppers bounce.

ShopGuide’s on-page AI agents are **proactive and guided**. 

They fuse real-time browsing behavior with intent signals (what the shopper is actually looking at, how they’re navigating, what attributes matter in the moment). Then they dynamically map the shortest, most relevant path through your catalog — surfacing the right products (including long-tail ones that usually stay buried) right on the page.

No leaving the site. No generic recommendations. No more “I know it’s here somewhere.”

This is the on-page difference merchants keep asking for: discovery that feels like a helpful expert walking the customer through the store, not a broken search bar.
