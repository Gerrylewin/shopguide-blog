## The Problem No Filter Fixes

You know the scene. Customer lands on your Shopify store with 5,000+ SKUs. They type something vague into search or click around filters. Results come back — too many, or none that fit what they actually need. They scroll, hesitate, and bounce.

Traditional search and filters put all the work on the shopper. They have to know the right keywords, understand your taxonomy, and navigate the decision tree themselves. In high-SKU stores, that rarely happens cleanly.

**Path Explorer** changes the equation. It sits inside the ShopGuide agent and turns passive browsing signals plus explicit intent into an active, guided path. It doesn't just show options — it figures out where the shopper is and what to do next.

## What Path Explorer Actually Does

It runs in the background of every session:

- Captures on-page behavior (views, filters used, dwell time, comparisons) and chat messages
- Parses intent with confidence scores
- Classifies the current stage (discovery, comparison, validation, ready to buy)
- Selects and delivers the best next action — clarifying question, focused shortlist, objection handling, or direct add-to-cart nudge
- Updates the path live as new signals arrive
- Records every step and outcome for your analytics

The result is fewer dead ends and more assisted purchases you can actually measure.

## How It Works Under the Hood

**Signal Capture**  
Pulls from Shopify storefront events and the chat interface. No extra tagging required beyond standard setup.

**Intent & Stage Engine**  
Matches signals to intent tags and scores journey stage in real time.

**Action Selector**  
Picks the highest-confidence move: recommend specific products, answer a compatibility question with proof, or push toward checkout when ready.

**Outcome Loop**  
Every action logs to dashboards. You see assisted conversion rate, revenue per guided session, AOV lift, time-to-cart, and objection resolution rates. Built for A/B testing and tuning.

It ties directly into your catalog, cart, reviews, and analytics — so recommendations stay accurate and actions feel native.

## Real Session Example

Shopper views several industrial parts, filters by specs, then chats "compatible with Series 900 blender?"

Path Explorer tags intent as technical validation + compatibility check. Classifies as Validation stage. Responds with fit data, price context, returns policy, and a short curated list of matching in-stock items. Logs the interaction.

If they buy → attributes assisted revenue. If they bounce → data for next iteration.

All measurable. All improvable.

## Merchant Reality Check

Merchants running large catalogs tell the same story: search and filters worked okay at 500 SKUs. At 5,000 or 50,000, they create more friction than clarity. Shoppers get overwhelmed or never find the exact match. Silent abandonment follows.

Path Explorer doesn't promise magic. It gives the agent the tools to act like a knowledgeable floor person who knows your inventory and asks the right follow-up at the right moment.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How is this different from product recommendations?**  
Standard recs are often static or based on past views. Path Explorer is live, stage-aware, and action-oriented. It decides _what to do next_ based on where the shopper actually is.

**Does it require custom code?**  
No. Standard Shopify API integration plus the ShopGuide agent handles it. Instrument events once; the platform does the rest.

**How do I see the numbers?**  
Dashboards track assisted CVR, revenue per assisted session, AOV delta, and more. A/B tests are native.

**What if confidence is low?**  
Safe fallbacks: clarifying questions or general guidance. High-impact actions stay behind confidence gates.

**Works for niche or long-tail products?**  
Yes. Intent signals match specific needs to exact items and complements that complete the purchase.
