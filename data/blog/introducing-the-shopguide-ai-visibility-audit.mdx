## The "Transactional Gap" is Killing Your Conversions

Most merchants think "optimization" ends at page load speed. In 2025, that's just the baseline.

The real threat is the **Transactional Gap**. This is the friction that occurs when an AI Agent tries to navigate your store, understand your product variations, and execute a purchase, but gets tripped up by poor technical infrastructure.

Our audit tool doesn't just look at "tags." It simulates how an autonomous agent interacts with your site.

## What We Scan (And Why It Matters)

When you run a scan, ShopGuide dives deep into four critical pillars:

1.  **AI Readiness & SEO Infrastructure:** Are you providing the right signals (JSON-LD, Structured Data) for LLMs to categorize your products correctly?
2.  **DNS & Server Health:** If your server flinches, an agent moves on. Speed is the currency of autonomy.
3.  **"Computer Use" Navigation:** We simulate how agents "see" your UI. If your buttons are ambiguous, the agent can't click "Add to Cart."
4.  **Transactional Friction:** We calculate the exact points where a user (or agent) is likely to drop off due to technical hurdles.
