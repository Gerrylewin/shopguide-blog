## The Hidden Cost of “Just Browsing”

Every merchant with a sizable catalog knows the frustration. A customer lands on your site, spends time clicking around, adds a few items… and then disappears. They were *interested*, but they got lost in the options.

Traditional tools — keyword search bars, faceted filters, “You might also like” widgets — put the burden on the shopper. They have to know exactly what they’re looking for, master your taxonomy, and navigate dozens of choices. In a 5,000+ SKU store, that’s not shopping. That’s detective work.

**Path Explorer** changes the game.

It’s the feature inside ShopGuide that turns passive browsing into an active, guided journey. Instead of waiting for customers to find their way, the AI agent actively maps the **shortest, highest-confidence path to purchase** based on real-time behavior and intent signals.

Think of it as replacing a confusing warehouse map with a helpful floor manager who knows exactly where you need to go and walks you there — asking smart clarifying questions only when needed.

## What Is Path Explorer?

Path Explorer is ShopGuide’s intent-mapping engine. It continuously observes how customers interact with your store — what they view, how long they linger, what they compare, what questions they ask — and combines that with explicit intent signals (natural language queries, refinements, cart additions).

The result? The agent doesn’t just recommend products. It constructs a **personalized guided path** that leads the customer from discovery to confident purchase with minimal friction.

## Core Features of Path Explorer

**1. Behavior + Intent Signal Fusion**  
It blends browsing patterns (time on page, scroll depth, comparisons) with conversational intent. This creates a rich, real-time understanding of what the customer actually wants.

**2. Dynamic Shortest-Path Mapping**  
Instead of static category pages or endless filter combinations, Path Explorer dynamically builds the optimal sequence of steps/products for that specific shopper. It prioritizes relevance and confidence at every turn.

**3. Guided Journey Orchestration**  
The agent doesn’t just show options — it guides. It can say: “Based on what you’ve looked at, here’s the best next step…” and then surface the exact product (or small curated set) that completes the journey, complete with clear explanations of *why* it fits.

**4. Real-Time Shopify API Integration**  
Everything stays live. Path Explorer pulls from the Shopify Catalog API (products, variants, inventory, metafields) so recommendations are always accurate. Out-of-stock items are gracefully avoided. New products become discoverable instantly.

**5. Long-Tail & Contextual Discovery**  
It excels at surfacing niche products that traditional search buries. A customer looking for “bulk flour for sourdough” gets steered toward the right specialty grain *and* the complementary items (yeast, proofing basket) that complete the project — naturally boosting AOV.
