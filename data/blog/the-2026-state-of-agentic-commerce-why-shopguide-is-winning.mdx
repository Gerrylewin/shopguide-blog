## 1. Support at the Speed of Slack

We know where you live. You live in Slack.

The biggest friction for merchants in 2025 was the "email silo." Support tickets would get buried in inboxes, leading to slow response times and frustrated customers.

In 2026, we’ve solved that. ShopGuide now features a **1-way Slack Integration**. When your AI Agent identifies a complex support ticket it can’t (or shouldn’t) handle autonomously, it sends a notification directly to the Slack channel of your choice.

- **Instant Visibility:** Your team sees the issue the moment it arises.
- **Workflow Integration:** Handle tickets where you already communicate.
- **Email Bypass:** If you love email, keep it. If you want speed, use Slack.

It’s about meeting merchants where they are, so they can meet customers where they need.
