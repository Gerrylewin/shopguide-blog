## The Scaling Trap

For growing [Shopify](https://shopify.pxf.io/eK6XxX) merchants, success often feels like a double-edged sword. As your catalog grows and your order volume spikes, so does your support queue.

Most high-volume stores find themselves trapped in a "Support Bleed"—hiring more agents just to keep up with repetitive queries. When you have thousands of SKUs, even "simple" product questions become complex for human staff to answer quickly.

## Beyond the Chatbot: Autonomous Resolution

Traditional chatbots are just glorified FAQ search engines. They point users to links, but they don't _do_ anything.

**ShopGuide is an Agentic Employee.** It integrates with your entire stack—**Judge.me** for reviews, [**Smile.io**](https://smile-io.typeform.com/to/U58RbKQD?utm_source=isaac) for loyalty, and **Klaviyo** for CRM—to provide resolutions, not just answers.

### 1. Autonomous Product Navigation

In a catalog with 50,000 SKUs, finding a specific variant is a needle-in-a-haystack problem. ShopGuide uses the [Shopify](https://shopify.pxf.io/eK6XxX) Catalog API to answer nuanced product questions instantly, resolving "Where is...?" tickets before they are even created.

### 2. The "Action Window" for Order Edits

The most common support ticket for high-volume stores is the post-purchase edit. ShopGuide features a configurable **Action Window**. You can set a time limit (e.g., 30 minutes) during which the AI agent is authorized to edit order details autonomously. No human intervention required.

### 3. Slack Handoff for High-Value Issues

While ShopGuide handles 70-80% of routine queries, it knows when to escalate. Through a 1-way Slack integration, your team gets notified only when a human touch is actually needed.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is an "Action Window" and how does it help reduce support tickets?**
The Action Window is a configurable time limit set in your ShopGuide dashboard. During this window, the AI agent has the authority to autonomously update order details like shipping addresses or product variants.

**Can I still talk to my customers if the AI is handling support?**
Absolutely. ShopGuide handles the "noise"—the 70% of repetitive, low-value questions. For complex or high-value interactions, ShopGuide uses a Slack notification system to alert your team.

**Does this integrate with my existing helpdesk like Gorgias?**
Yes. ShopGuide works alongside your existing tools, acting as the first line of defense. By resolving the majority of tickets autonomously, it allows your helpdesk agents to focus on high-priority issues.
