## Stop Burning Your List

It’s 2026. If you’re still sending the same "10% Off Everything" blast to your entire Klaviyo list, you’re not marketing—you’re spamming.

The most valuable data in your store isn't what people _bought_ six months ago. It’s what they are **talking about right now**.

ShopGuide’s new Klaviyo integration bridges the gap between the on-site conversation and the inbox. We’ve turned "intent" into "actionable data points."

---

## 1. Intent-Based Segmentation (The "Magic" Link)

When a customer talks to your ShopGuide AI Agent, they are revealing their deepest desires (or at least what kind of shoes they want for summer).

Previously, that data stayed in the chat bubble. Now, ShopGuide pushes those specific intent signals directly into Klaviyo as **Custom Events**.

- **Example:** A customer asks, "Do you have these in vegan leather?"
- **The Action:** ShopGuide tags that profile in Klaviyo with `Interest: Vegan`.
- **The Result:** Your next "New Arrival" email for vegan products goes _only_ to the people who actually care. Open rates double. Unsubscribes drop.

## 2. Dynamic Follow-Ups That Actually Convert

We’ve all seen the "You left something in your cart" email. They’re boring.

With the ShopGuide integration, your Klaviyo flows can now reference the **exact advice** the agent gave.

Instead of: _"Hey, you forgot these shoes,"_
Try: _"Hey, the ShopGuide Agent mentioned these run a bit small—still interested in the size 10?"_

It’s personalized, it’s helpful, and it feels human. That’s how you win in 2026.

## 3. LTV is a Conversation, Not a Transaction

The goal of this integration isn't just the first sale. It’s the next ten. By feeding AI-driven insights into your Klaviyo CRM, you’re building a profile of your customer that no generic tracker can match.

You aren't just selling products; you’re managing a relationship. And in the world of Agentic Commerce, the best relationship wins.
