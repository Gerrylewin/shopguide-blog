For a decade, e-commerce has been a game of "who has the biggest ad budget?" If you could outspend your competition on Google or Meta, you won the discovery game. But in 2026, the rules have fundamentally changed.

We have entered the **Agentic Era**.

## What is "Merit-Based Shopping"?

In a recent interview with [_Retail Brew_](https://www.retailbrew.com/stories/2026/01/16/shopify-president-agentic-commerce-could-usher-in-merit-based-shopping), Finkelstein introduced a concept that should make every small-to-medium merchant cheer: **Merit-Based Shopping**.

Traditional search results are often skewed by SEO hacks and massive ad spend. AI Agents, however, don't care about your CPC. They care about **intent** and **fit**.

If a customer asks their agent for "the best eco-friendly running shoes for marathon training," the agent won't just show the brand that paid for the top spot. It will scour the **Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP)**—the new open standard co-developed by Google and Shopify—to find the product that actually meets the criteria.

## The Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP)

Announced in January 2026, the **UCP** is the infrastructure that makes this possible. It connects AI agents directly to merchants, allowing them to:

- Access real-time inventory and pricing.
- Apply loyalty points and discount codes.
- Execute checkouts across different platforms seamlessly.

If your store isn't "indexed" for agents, you are effectively invisible to the fastest-growing segment of shoppers.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What exactly is an "Agentic" transaction?**
An agentic transaction is one where an AI Agent (like ShopGuide or a personal shopping assistant) handles the discovery, comparison, and execution of a purchase on behalf of a human.

**Is Agentic Commerce replacing e-commerce sites?**
No. As Harley Finkelstein puts it, your online store remains the "hub" for storytelling and brand identity. Agents are a new "spoke" that increases the reach and penetration of your store.

**How does "Merit-Based Shopping" help small brands?**
It removes the "ad spend tax." Small brands with superior products can be discovered by AI agents based on their actual quality and relevance to a customer's specific needs, rather than how much they spent on keywords.

**How do I get my [Shopify](https://shopify.pxf.io/eK6XxX) store ready for AI agents?**
